MUSIC GALATTA

Rules

  • The game consists of three rounds
  • A maximum of three members can be in a team
  • Participants should have mobile phones with proper internet and Bluetooth connectivity
  • The first two rounds involve manual buzzer rounds
  • The last round will be conducted manually
  • The team that finds the maximum number of songs will be declared the winner

Round Details

First Round: Screen Quest

  • A set of pictures which describes the song lyrics will be displayed in the ppt
  • The participants will be given a buzzer each
  • The participants will have to identify the song within a time limit
  • The first participant to identify the song by buzzing in will be asked to answer
  • If the answer is correct, the participant will be awarded a point
  • If the answer is wrong, the participant will lose the point and the next team gets a chance
  • The top performers will be advanced to the next round

Second Round: Lyrics Unplugged

  • A set of song lyrics will be shown but with some missing lines
  • The participants will be given a buzzer each
  • The participants will have to identify the missing lyrics within a time limit
  • The first participant to identify the song by buzzing in will be asked to answer
  • If the answer is correct, the participant will be awarded a point
  • If the answer is wrong, the participant will lose the point and the next team gets a chance
  • The top performers will be advanced to the next round

Third Round: Silent Clues

  • One person from the team will receive a song. That person should act and make his/her team members find the song
  • The participants will have to identify the song within a time limit
  • If the answer is correct, the participant will be awarded a point
  • If the answer is wrong, the participant will lose the point
  • The participants with the maximum number of points at the end of the round will be declared as winners

Note: The organiser's decision will be final in case of any disputes.
